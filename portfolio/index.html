Walk Georgia keyboard_arrow_down
Beleive it or not, computers can help you exercise too! I was hired by the University of Georgia to do a complete design overhaul of the Walk Georgia program in partnership with the Coca-Cola Foundation. In addition to the design and development work, our team of two was responsible for a technical overhaul of the fitness tracking system. We developed a conversion formula that could accurately compare unrelated activities (yoga and swimming) and then award points to the user based on time, difficulty, etc. After being promoted, it was then my responsibility to assit in hiring another designer to maintain and continue the program.

Note: Due to the policy of UGA, this repo has to stay private. But trust me, it's a great repo.
